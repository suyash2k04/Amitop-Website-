Established in 2010, Amitop (formerly Optima Life Sciences) has grown into a leading provider of animal health and nutrition solutions. We partner with premier international principals to bring world-class feed additives, bio-security products, and dosing systems to the global market.

Our team of veterinarians, nutritionists, and engineers works relentlessly to ensure every solution delivers a defined return on investment — improving feed conversion ratios, reducing mortality, and enhancing overall farm profitability.

With a robust supply chain and technical support network spanning Asia, Africa, Europe, and the Americas, we guarantee reliable delivery and on-ground service excellence.
